## The offer (the hero product = the AI Quoting Agent)

simple questions, gives the caller an **instant ballpark quote in ~15 seconds** off the
owner's OWN pricing, then books the on-site estimate straight into the calendar and

scheduling agents are later upsells, not the headline.

## Core pain it solves (make them FEEL this)
- HVAC/roofing jobs go to whoever responds first. Right now that takes days: call →
  voicemail → callback → measure → written quote. Every hour of that gap is a competitor's opening.
- Owners miss calls while on a roof or under a unit. A missed call doesn't leave a
  voicemail — the caller just dials the next company. That's not a missed call, it's a lost job.
- After-hours calls (9pm, AC dies) die in voicemail and go to the competitor who answers.

- Avg job at stake: HVAC replacement $7k-20k · roof replacement $9k-18k. One saved job pays for months.
